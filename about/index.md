## Principal Investigator

<div class="team-member">
<h3>Dr. Martin G. Frasch, MD, PhD</h3>
<p>Martin is a neuroscientist focused on improving perinatal health outcomes by studying the brain's responses to asphyxia, inflammation/infection, and stress. His research applies machine learning techniques for signal analysis and AI frameworks to convert medical data into physiological phenotypes with predictive potential.</p>
<p>His work emphasizes non-invasive monitoring technologies (ECG, EEG) to identify at-risk babies. His preclinical research examines how prenatal harmful events shape brain development, particularly regarding glial biology, microglia, and astrocytes&mdash;relevant to neuroinflammatory and neurodegenerative diseases like Multiple Sclerosis and Alzheimer's.</p>
