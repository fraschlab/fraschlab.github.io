# Public Data Sets, Protocols & Code

The Frasch Lab is committed to open science. We make our research data, protocols, and code available to the broader scientific community.

---

## Featured Data Sets

- **Fetal Sheep In Vivo Model** -- Physiological data from a near-term ovine fetus model. Includes ECG, EEG, and other vital signals.
  [View on PubMed Central](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC4692673/)

- **Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) ECG** -- ECG data used for the detection of autism spectrum disorder, along with associated code.
  [View on GitHub](https://github.com/martinfrasch/ASD_project)

- **Synchronous Fetal & Maternal ECG** -- A dataset of simultaneously recorded fetal and maternal sheep ECGs.
  [View on Harvard Dataverse](https://dataverse.harvard.edu/dataset.xhtml?persistentId=doi:10.7910/DVN/NGZOPC)

- **Ovine Fetal Microglia RNAseq Data** -- RNAseq data for microglia from our ovine fetal models.
  [View on GEO (GSE101857)](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/geo/query/acc.cgi?acc=GSE101857)

- **Ovine Fetal Astrocyte RNAseq Data** -- RNAseq data for astrocytes from our ovine fetal models.
  [View on GEO (GSE123713)](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/geo/query/acc.cgi?acc=GSE123713) |
  [Code on GitHub](https://github.com/martinfrasch/astrocytes_RNAseq)

- **Cerebral Ischemia Pig Model** -- A multimodal dataset from a pig model of controlled, gradual cerebral ischemia.
  [View on FigShare](https://figshare.com/articles/dataset/Corticothalamic_communication_under_analgesia_sedation_and_gradual_ischemia_a_multimodal_model_of_controlled_gradual_cerebral_ischemia_in_pig/7834442/10)

- **Cerebral Ischemia in Pig Model: Evoked Potentials** -- Update with auditory, sensory, and high-frequency evoked potentials.
  [View on FigShare](https://figshare.com/articles/dataset/Update_on_the_multimodal_pathophysiological_dataset_of_gradual_cerebral_ischemia_in_a_cohort_of_juvenile_pigs_auditory_sensory_and_high-frequency_sensory_evoked_potentials/14122049)

- **VENG in Piglets (Septic Shock)** -- ECG and vagus nerve electroneurogram (VENG) recordings in piglets subjected to sublethal septic shock.
  [View on FigShare](https://figshare.com/articles/dataset/The_neonatal_sepsis_is_diminished_by_cervical_vagus_nerve_stimulation_and_tracked_non-invasively_by_ECG_a_preliminary_report_in_the_piglet_model_with_cervical_vagus_electroneurograms/7098809)

## Protocols & Methods

- **Fetal Sheep In Vivo Protocol** -- Detailed methods for instrumentation of near-term fetal sheep for multivariate chronic non-anesthetized monitoring.
  [View on JoVE](https://app.jove.com/t/52581/instrumentation-near-term-fetal-sheep-for-multivariate-chronic-non)

- **In Vivo -- In Vitro Multi-Hit Model of Inflammation** -- Protocol to derive primary microglia and astrocyte cultures from our fetal sheep model after an inflammatory challenge.
  [View on protocols.io](https://www.protocols.io/view/manipulation-of-7-nicotinic-acetylcholine-receptor-yxmvm94bbl3p/v1)

- **VENG Approach in Fetal Sheep** -- Methods for cervical vagotomy and electroneurogram (VENG) recording in near-term fetal sheep.
  [View on FigShare](https://figshare.com/articles/media/Cervical_vagotomy_and_electroneurogram_VENG_approach_in_near-term_fetal_sheep/7228307)
